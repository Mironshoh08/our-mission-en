Our mission

We have developed a set of principles for running our business by being a reliable and stable company in the Republic of Uzbekistan. We strive to support our customers, our suppliers, contractors and service providers in superiority and sustainable solutions and provide various types of chemicals to improve productivity in the water industry. Our conviction lies in the rational management of water resources. We conduct business in Uzbekistan, observing the requirements of the law and the Code of Business Conduct and Ethics. And it is not just words. Day by day, we implement programs that help us to solve, ethically and honestly, business issues.

The head office is located in Uzbekistan, Chirchik. PWC was founded in 2017 and has significant presence in Central Asia.
